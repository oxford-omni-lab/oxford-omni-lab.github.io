Team

Our group was founded in 2017 by Dr. Ana Namburete with the aim of improving fetal brain assessment using machine learning and neuroimaging. We are a collaborative and open-minded group of researchers, eager to push the frontiers of biomedical imaging. For more about our lab culture, please see our lab handbook.

We are always looking for new DPhil/MSc students and Postdocs to join the team !
