Together with our collaborators we have published two new papers in _Nature Communications_ and _The Lancet Obstetrics, Gynaecology, and Women’s Health_ on fetal brain mapping and neurodevelopmental outcomes at 2 years of age.

**Read the paper _Normative growth trajectories of fetal brain regions validated by satisfactory maturation of neurodevelopmental domains at 2 years of age_.**: [{DOI 10.1038/s41467-026-69657-w}](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-026-69657-w).
