Siân Wilson joined our group as a Postdoc from Developmental Science Centre at Boston Children’s Hospital and Harvard Medical School. She will be working on prenatal brain development and early life outcomes. She is collaborating closely with the Department of Women's Health to explore how maternal factors influence fetal brain growth.
