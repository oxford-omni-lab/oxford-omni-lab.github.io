### About Me

I am a student on the [Healthcare Data Science CDT](https://www.bdi.ox.ac.uk/study/cdt). As one of my rotation projects I am joining the OMNI Lab to investigate techniques for extending fetal brain segmentation tools into the third trimester.

### Background

I completed an integrated master's degree in Natural Sciences at the University of Exeter in 2025. My master’s project investigated the neonatal brain’s response to noxious stimuli using topographical analysis of EEG data.

During this time, I also took part in the Summer Research Program at EPFL. Working in the Laboratory of Psychophysics, I explored whether current deep neural networks align with human performance in visual reasoning tasks involving continuity grouping.
